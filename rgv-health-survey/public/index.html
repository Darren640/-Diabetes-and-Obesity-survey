Rio Grande Valley Health Survey

Hello I am a college student conducting a survey to better understand perspective on obesity and diabetes in the Rio Grande Valley, particularly among high school and college students. The purpose of this survey is to gather information about health awareness, lifestyle habits, and access to resources within the community.

Participation in this survey is completely voluntary. You may choose to stop at any time. The survey should take approximately five minutes to complete.

Your respones will be used solely for a course research project and will not be shared publicly or used for any other purpose. All respones are anonymous.

Thank you for your time and participation.
